Kid Chameleon uses the GEMS sound driver.

Informational video:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WEvnZRCW_qc

Possible(?) options to edit music:
- Use gems_split and gems_combine to replace songs with e.g. songs from other games
- Set up the GEMS music editor in Dosbox and compose music
- Use GEMS2ASM and hand-edit music in a text editor
- ?
